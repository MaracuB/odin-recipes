Cardamom Coffee

Description

This is so easy you cannot even call it a recipe! The combination is delicious though that I had to include it

Ingredients

  • Filter coffee or your choice of (black) coffee
  • Milk
  • Cardamom pod(s)
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Brew coffee
  2. Add in milk and/or sugar
  3. Lightly crush cardamom pod(s), then add them to your coffee
