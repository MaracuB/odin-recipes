Saag Paneer

Description

Saag paneer is a delicious, healthy, Indian dish. It is mainly made from onions, tomatoes, spinach and paneer (a type of Indian cheese). You can eat paneer as a side dish, or with rice, or naan, whatever you prefer!

It is not very hard to make, but it is important to use tasty spinach. If you like, you can also blend your spinach mixture in a blender to ensure a smoother texture

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Add oil or ghee to your pan, when it is hot, add in the spices and wait for them to sizzle
  2. Add onions, brown them
  3. Once the onions have a nice colour, add in the ginger garlic paste. Stir until the raw smell of the paste goes away
  4. Add in tomatoes, wait until soft
  5. Add in dry spices
  6. Add in paneer, cover for 2 minutes
